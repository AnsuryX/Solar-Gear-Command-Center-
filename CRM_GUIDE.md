# 📊 Command CRM & Strategic Project Orchestration

Welcome to the Nexus Command CRM. This module replaces a traditional project management office (PMO) by using AI to architect tactical action decks from strategic signals.

## 🚀 Initializing a Signal (Project)

1. Navigate to **Command CRM** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Initialize Project**.
3. Provide a **Project Title** (e.g., "Q3 Expansion Nairobi") and an **Executive Goal**.
4. **Autonomous Architecting:** Upon deployment, Nexus AI analyzes your goal and automatically generates an **Action Deck** (5-7 tactical tasks) relevant to the solar industry.

## 📋 Task Orchestration

- **Priority Synthesis:** Each task is assigned a priority (High, Medium, Low) based on the AI's estimation of impact vs. complexity.
- **Cycle Completion:** Toggle task completion to update your project progress.
- **Re-Generation:** If a project pivot occurs, use the "Re-generate Action Deck" button to synthesize a new tactical path.

## 🧠 Strategic Context

Nexus uses your **Brand Voice** and **Tone** (configured in **Nexus Bridge & Config**) to ensure that all suggested tasks align with your executive positioning.

## 🛡 Security

All CRM data is stored securely in your dedicated Firebase instance. Ensure your security rules are deployed to maintain exclusive access.
